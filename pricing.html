Common Questions

Answers Before You Book.

What's the minimum commitment?
Three months. AI and automation systems need time to compound — month-one results are rarely the full picture. After the initial three months, all engagements are month-to-month. No long contracts, no auto-renewals you can't get out of.
Is there a setup fee on packages?
No. Foundation, Growth, and Scale packages include all onboarding, setup, integrations, and asset builds. Setup fees only apply to certain à la carte modular services (AI Dispatch Setup, CRM Integration).
Do you guarantee results?
We commit to clear KPIs at the start of every engagement — lead volume, cost per booked job, close rate, attributed revenue. If we miss the agreed-on metrics, we work for free until we hit them. We don't guarantee vanity numbers we can't control (like Google rankings on day one) — but we guarantee accountability on what actually moves your business.
Can I switch tiers later?
Yes. Upgrade or downgrade at the end of any billing cycle. Most clients start at Foundation or Growth and move up as their revenue grows.
Do ad spend dollars go through Leapify Media?
No. You keep your own ad accounts and pay platforms (Google, Meta) directly. Our fee is purely for strategy, execution, and infrastructure. This keeps your data and accounts yours forever — even if you ever decide to leave us.
Do you work with non-home-service businesses?
No. We're specialists. HVAC, roofing, plumbing, landscaping & turf, restoration, and adjacent home-service verticals only. The "real engineers who understand the business" pitch requires staying focused on what we actually understand.
How does the Enterprise performance-based component work?
On Enterprise engagements ($25K+/mo, typically with operators above $10M revenue), part of the engagement is a fixed monthly retainer and part is tied to attributed revenue above a baseline you set. The logic: if we're helping you scale from $13M to $20M+, our team and infrastructure investment grows alongside that — the performance component lets us sustain that investment without inflating the base fee. Exact structure (baseline, percentage, exclusions) is negotiated per engagement.
