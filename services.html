02
On-Premise AI Dispatch

Hot Leads To Your Closer In 90 Seconds.

Every lead is not equal. The homeowner Googling "emergency HVAC repair Austin" at 11pm is worth ten times the casual form-fill from a Sunday afternoon ad click. Treating them identically is how most operators waste their closers' time.

Our on-premise AI dispatch reads every incoming lead in real time, scores intent based on the actual signals (source, page behavior, urgency language, demographics, job size predictors), and routes accordingly. Hot leads get a human in 90 seconds. Warm leads enter tailored nurture. Cold leads go to long-term drip without burning your team.

The AI runs on hardware we own. Not OpenAI. Not Anthropic. Not Google. Your customer data, pricing, and trade secrets never leave our infrastructure.

  • Local LLM inference (Llama/Hermes class models on Leapify-owned hardware)
  • Multi-signal intent scoring engine tuned to your vertical
  • Automated routing rules (hot → closer, warm → nurture, cold → drip)
  • Integration with your existing FSM (Jobber, Housecall, ServiceTitan, etc.)
  • Dedicated server instance — one client per machine on Scale tier

03
CRM Setup & Automation

Stop Losing Leads In Your Inbox.

The number-one revenue leak in home services isn't bad ads — it's leads that came in, sat in someone's inbox or voicemail, and went cold before anyone responded. We wire the connective tissue that ensures every lead, call, and quote gets the follow-up sequence that closes it.

If you're already on a Field Service Management tool (Jobber, Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan, etc.), we keep it. We add the marketing layer on top — CRM workflows in GoHighLevel, automated email/SMS sequences, review automation, retention campaigns, and the integration glue that hands booked jobs cleanly from your marketing systems to your operations team.

  • GoHighLevel agency setup, white-labeled to look like Leapify Media
  • FSM integration with Jobber, Housecall Pro, ServiceTitan, or your tool
  • Multi-channel inbox (SMS, email, web chat, Facebook, Instagram all in one view)
  • Quote follow-up automation — recover the $$$ pipeline that goes silent
  • Review automation — auto-request after every completed job
  • Retention campaigns for past clients (seasonal, birthday, reactivation)

04
Content Production

Build Trust Before
The First Call.

Stock footage and generic AI b-roll don't sell home service. Real installs, real customers, real owners on real job sites — that's what builds trust before a prospect ever picks up the phone. We come on-site with cinema-grade cameras, lighting, and an FAA-licensed drone pilot.

Two production cadences: Quarterly Pro Production Days for premium assets (testimonials, owner-on-camera, aerial drone, brand sizzle), and ongoing content management where your team shoots daily phone footage and we edit it into brand-consistent social cuts. Best of both: scalable polish + authentic volume.

  • Half-Day or Full-Day Pro Production with cinema cameras + drone
  • Brand sizzle reels, owner testimonials, install b-roll, customer reviews on-camera
  • Multi-format edits (long-form, vertical Reels/Shorts/TikTok, photo sets)
  • Ongoing editing of your team's daily phone content into branded social posts
  • You keep all raw footage and final videos forever
