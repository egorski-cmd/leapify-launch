Before The Call

Three Things To Bring.

No prep doc, no questionnaire. Just have these ready in your head — the call is way more useful when you do.

01

Your Real Numbers

Average ticket. Close rate. Monthly ad spend. Cost per lead if you know it. We don't need a spreadsheet — ballparks are fine. We just need enough to do honest math with you.

02

The Unfiltered Picture

What's actually working. What's actually broken. What you'd never put in a LinkedIn post. The more polished spin you bring, the less useful the next 30 minutes will be.

03

A Laptop, Not A Phone

We'll likely screen-share — your site, your CRM, your ad account. Phone calls work in a pinch but you'll get a lot more out of being on a real screen.

What We'll Cover

30 Minutes. Real Work.

No slide deck. No "let me tell you about Leapify Media." You already know what we do — that's why you booked. The whole call is about you.

Live Funnel Audit

We'll walk through your acquisition, intake, and follow-up systems in real time — site, ads, CRM, dispatch. You see what we see.

The Biggest Leak

The #1 thing costing you booked jobs right now — named, quantified, and ranked against what else is broken. The thing we'd fix first if you were a client.

Honest Fit Check

Whether we're the right team for your stage of business. If we're not, we'll tell you that — and point you toward what would actually move the needle.

If Something Changes

Life Happens. Just Give Us A Heads-Up.
